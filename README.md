# DLocal Limited (NASDAQ: DLO) — Equity Research

**Rating: SELL | Price Target: $8.50 | Current Price: $11.61 | Implied Downside: (27%)**

---

## Investment Thesis

DLocal operates payments infrastructure across emerging markets, processing $25.6Bn in TPV in 2024 across 40+ countries. Despite strong volume growth, three structural headwinds support a SELL recommendation:

- **Take Rate Compression** — Net take rate has declined from 1.9% (2022) to 1.2% (2024) as competition intensifies in Brazil and Mexico, DLocal's core markets. We model further compression to ~0.9% by 2027E, capping revenue upside despite TPV growth of 40%+ near-term.
- **Operating Leverage Not Materializing** — G&A expenses grew 43% in 2024 versus revenue growth of 15%. Operating margin contracted from 31% (2022) to 19% (2024). Cost discipline remains the key execution risk and is not yet visible in the numbers.
- **Valuation Offers No Margin of Safety** — At $11.61, DLocal trades at 4.6x EV/Revenue versus a peer median of 2.5x. Our DCF yields an intrinsic value of $8.33/share with limited near-term catalysts to close the gap.

## Key Risks to Thesis

**Upside Risks:** Take rate stabilization or faster expansion into Africa and Southeast Asia could drive revenue materially above base case. Margin improvement from operating leverage would compress the valuation gap.

**Downside Risks:** Macro deterioration in Brazil and Argentina (60%+ of TPV) or further FX volatility could impair cash flows beyond our bear case. Regulatory changes to cross-border payment frameworks remain a structural risk.
